# Week 13 — Google Looker Studio, Analytics e Google Sites

Questa settimana ho iniziato il modulo **Looker Studio**, lo strumento di Business Intelligence e reporting di Google. Mi sono concentrato su due ambiti:

1.  **Il monitoraggio della presenza online**: introduzione a **Google Analytics** per il tracciamento del traffico web e a **Google Sites** per la creazione di siti web no-code.
2.  **La creazione di report e dashboard**: uso di **Google Looker Studio** per importare dati, analizzarli e visualizzarli in report interattivi e condivisibili.

Successivamente, mi sono immerso in Looker Studio, imparando a connettere varie fonti dati, a unirle tramite *data blending*, a eseguire un'analisi esplorativa (EDA) e a creare visualizzazioni efficaci con controlli e filtri interattivi.

*   Progettare report interattivi con filtri incrociati tra grafici

## Sfide risolte

*   Comprendere la differenza tra *data source* e *connettore* in Looker Studio
*   Configurare correttamente una connessione a un database SQL tramite query personalizzata
*   Impostare un'*unione* (blend) di dati tra fonti eterogenee con condizioni di join corrette
*   Selezionare il tipo di grafico più adatto per comunicare un insight specifico
*   Gestire la gerarchia e l'ambito d'azione dei filtri e dei controlli all'interno di un report

# Week 13 — Google Looker Studio, Analytics, and Google Sites

This week I started the **Looker Studio** module, Google's Business Intelligence and reporting tool. I focused on two areas:

1.  **Online presence monitoring**: introduction to **Google Analytics** for web traffic tracking and to **Google Sites** for no-code website creation.
2.  **Report and dashboard creation**: use of **Google Looker Studio** to import, analyze, and visualize data in interactive, shareable reports.

I learned the importance of online presence and how to measure it, how to build a personal site and connect it to analytics. Subsequently, I immersed myself in Looker Studio, learning to connect various data sources, combine them via *data blending*, perform exploratory analysis (EDA), and create effective visualizations with interactive controls and filters.

## Skills developed

*   Understanding the role of Google Analytics in web monitoring
*   Creating and publishing a website with Google Sites and connecting it to Google Analytics
*   Understanding the Looker Studio ecosystem and interface
*   Connecting Looker Studio to various data sources (Google Analytics, Google Sheets, SQL databases)
*   Performing *data blending* to merge tables from different origins
*   Creating basic and advanced charts for exploratory data analysis (EDA)
*   Implementing interactive controls (dropdown filters, date selectors) and static filters
*   Designing interactive reports with cross-filtering between charts

## Challenges overcome

*   Understanding the difference between a *data source* and a *connector* in Looker Studio
*   Correctly configuring a connection to an SQL database via custom query
*   Setting up a data *blend* between heterogeneous sources with correct join conditions
*   Selecting the most suitable chart type to communicate a specific insight
*   Managing the hierarchy and scope of filters and controls within a report
